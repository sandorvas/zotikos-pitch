# Core Message

## The Problem

Most AI solutions fail in banking environments not because of model quality, but because they lack:

- governance
- auditability
- control over lifecycle
- integration with enterprise systems

AI becomes a risk instead of an asset.

---

## The Insight

Banks do not need more models.

They need:

→ controlled, observable, and governed AI systems  
→ that behave like production infrastructure  

---

## The Solution

We propose a:

**Control-Plane Driven AI Architecture**

- Event-driven orchestration
- Finite State Machine (FSM) lifecycle control
- Full audit trail in BigQuery
- Separation of:
  - control plane (decision, orchestration)
  - serving plane (models, inference)

---

## The Outcome

- AI systems become **traceable and controllable**
- Regulatory compliance becomes **built-in**
- Failures are **observable and recoverable**
- Deployment becomes **repeatable and safe**
